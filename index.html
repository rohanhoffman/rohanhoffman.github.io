About Me

Hi! I'm Rohan, a 3rd year Mechanical Engineering student at UC Irvine. I'm passionate about sustainable energy systems, energy storage, electrochemical engineering, and bringing creative engineering solutions to life.

I currently conduct undergraduate research with the Zenyuk Group, focusing on contact resistance and hot-pressing for AEM technology, and serve as a Project Coordinator for Engineers for a Sustainable World at UCI. I am also working with Dr. David Copp to conduct engineering education research, focusing on AI and self-efficacy with undergraducate engineering students.

Previously, I worked on a team with UCI's combat engineering team on a robot, and I was a Research Engineer Intern at USC Viterbi. I'm always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as an engineer.
